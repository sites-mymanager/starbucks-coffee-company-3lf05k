About Us

A new era of coffee craftsmanship

For over 50 years, Starbucks has been more than just coffee – we've been a place where communities gather, stories unfold, and connections are made. From our humble beginnings in Seattle's Pike Place Market to becoming a global phenomenon, our passion for exceptional coffee and human connection drives everything we do.

Every cup we serve is a testament to our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the artistry of coffee. We source the finest beans from coffee-growing regions around the world, roast them to perfection, and craft beverages that inspire moments of connection and joy in your everyday life.
